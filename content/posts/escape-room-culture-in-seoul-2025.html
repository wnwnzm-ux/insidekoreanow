🔐 Experience the Thrill of Escape Rooms in Seoul

Looking for something exciting to do in Seoul besides shopping and sightseeing? Try an escape room — known in Korean as 방탈출. This interactive game challenges you to solve puzzles, crack codes, and find your way out before time runs out. It’s a mix of mystery, teamwork, and adrenaline — and yes, some escape rooms in Seoul now offer English-language options for foreign players!

🎭 What Makes Escape Rooms in Seoul Special?

Seoul’s escape room culture is on another level. These aren’t just puzzles in a room — they’re immersive story worlds designed with movie-like production. Themes range from spy missions and haunted hospitals to bank heists and time travel adventures. Each room is carefully built with props, lighting, and sound effects that make you feel like you’re inside a film set.

Most experiences last about 60 minutes and are perfect for friends, couples, and team-building groups. Even locals say it’s one of the most addictive weekend activities in Korea!
