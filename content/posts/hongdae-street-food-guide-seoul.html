Discover the Best Street Foods in Hongdae

Hongdae is one of Seoul’s liveliest neighborhoods — a fusion of music, youth, and street food. Just a few steps from Hongdae Station, you’ll find endless food stalls offering everything from crispy snacks to sweet desserts. Let’s explore the most popular street foods and must-visit spots in this trendy area!

🍡 Hongdae’s Most Popular Street Foods

From savory skewers to sweet pastries, Hongdae’s street food scene never disappoints. Here are some of the top picks loved by locals and tourists alike.

🍢 Dakkochi (Grilled Chicken Skewers)

Ppilkkkochine Hongdae (삘꼬치네 홍대) is famous for its juicy grilled chicken skewers. The smoky aroma and spicy glaze make it one of Hongdae’s all-time favorite street snacks — especially popular during cool evenings when the streets come alive with music and lights.

🍞 Sweet & Savory Breads

  • Seoul Hotteok (서울호떡) – A must-try sweet pancake filled with brown sugar and nuts, perfect for a cozy snack.
  • 10 Won Bread (이쩜오 홍대점) – This trendy mini bread is soft, fluffy, and filled with sweet cream. It’s gone viral on social media for its cute coin-like shape.
  • Croissant Bungeoppang – A modern twist on Korea’s classic fish-shaped pastry, this version uses croissant dough for an extra crispy texture. Popular in both Hongdae and Myeongdong.

🍥 Other Local Favorites

  • Moon Skewers (문꼬치) – A fun, chewy treat grilled to perfection.
  • Tteokgalbi Meatballs (떡갈비 완자) – Juicy Korean meatballs served on sticks; you can also find them in Myeongdong.
  • Fried Banana (바나나 튀김) – Sweet and crispy, a favorite dessert option after a night out in Hongdae.
  • Tteokbokki (떡볶이) – Korea’s ultimate street food, made with rice cakes simmered in spicy gochujang sauce, often served with fish cakes and boiled eggs.
  • Crepes & Pancakes – PINKS Crepe Pancake Hongdae and Dalkkot Crepe (달꽃크레페 홍대2호점) are perfect for dessert lovers. Try their Nutella Banana Crepe — a favorite in both Hongdae and Myeongdong.

📍 Must-Visit Street Food Spots Near Hongdae Station

For an unforgettable foodie experience, don’t miss these popular locations near Hongdae Entrance Station:

  • K1 35cm Hongdae – Famous for its giant 35cm-long skewers filled with meat, cheese, and vegetables.
  • Luna Pie Hongdae – Offers warm, freshly baked mini pies in various flavors — from apple cinnamon to chocolate cream.
  • Wangga Tanghulu Hongdae – Known for shiny, crystal-coated fruit skewers that are as pretty as they are delicious.
  • 1004 Donut Hongdae – A must-stop for dessert lovers, featuring freshly fried donuts and seasonal toppings.

💡 Final Tips for Food Lovers

Hongdae’s street food scene captures the youthful, creative energy of Seoul. Visit in the evening for the best atmosphere, when lights glow and food stalls line the streets. Whether you’re craving spicy, sweet, or savory, Hongdae has something for every taste bud.
