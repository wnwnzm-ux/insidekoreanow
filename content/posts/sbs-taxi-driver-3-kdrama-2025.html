🚖 The Return of Justice — Taxi Driver 3 Premieres This November

The beloved SBS drama series “Taxi Driver” is making its much-awaited comeback with Season 3, premiering on November 21, 2025. Following the success of its first two seasons, this action-packed revenge thriller returns to deliver more intense, emotional stories centered around justice for the powerless. If you haven’t watched Seasons 1 and 2 yet — now’s the perfect time to catch up!

🌆 A Story of Revenge, Justice, and Redemption

Based on the popular webtoon, Taxi Driver portrays the mysterious taxi company Rainbow Transport, which takes justice into its own hands when the law fails. Led by Kim Do-gi (played by Lee Je-hoon), the team avenges victims of injustice through daring, strategic missions that leave wrongdoers trembling. The show’s mix of crime, action, and moral storytelling has made it one of SBS’s most compelling dramas in recent years.

🔥 Season 1 — The Beginning of the Ride (2021)

  • Broadcast Period: April 9 – May 29, 2021
  • Starring: Lee Je-hoon, Esom
  • Production: Studio S & Group Eight
  • What Made It Special: Season 1 introduced audiences to the dark yet thrilling world of private revenge. Each episode tackled real-world issues — from bullying to fraud — giving viewers a sense of cathartic justice. Fans praised the drama’s sharp storytelling and emotional impact.

💬 Viewer Tip: Watch Season 1 to understand Kim Do-gi’s tragic past and how Rainbow Transport came together — it’s the emotional core of the entire series.

⚡ Season 2 — Bigger Missions, Deeper Emotions (2023)

  • Broadcast Period: February 17 – April 15, 2023
  • Genre: Crime · Action · Thriller (Rated 15+)
  • Highlights: Season 2 raised the stakes with global-scale missions, tighter team dynamics, and jaw-dropping twists. Critics called it “more polished and emotionally charged” than Season 1. Lee Je-hoon’s acting received rave reviews for balancing cold vengeance with human compassion.

💬 Viewer Tip: If you loved K-dramas like Vincenzo or The Glory, you’ll be hooked by Taxi Driver 2 — it combines moral dilemmas with stylish action.

🚨 Season 3 — The Most Anticipated Return of 2025

  • Premiere Date: November 21, 2025 (Fridays & Saturdays at 9:50 p.m. on SBS)
  • Episodes: 16 total
  • Genre: Action · Crime · Revenge · Social Commentary
  • Cast & Crew: Lee Je-hoon returns as Kim Do-gi, with Studio S, Group Eight, and BA Entertainment leading production. Directed by Kang Bo-seung and written by Oh Sang-ho, the season promises even more heart-pounding missions and moral questions about justice.

In Season 3, Kim Do-gi will face new enemies and even greater personal trials. The teaser hints at darker tones and higher emotional stakes, proving that this installment could be the most powerful yet.

🎬 Why You Should Watch “Taxi Driver” Before Season 3

Both Seasons 1 and 2 have been praised for their bold storytelling, emotional depth, and realistic portrayal of social injustices in Korea. The show’s ability to mix revenge, empathy, and redemption makes it a standout among modern K-dramas. Whether you’re a fan of thrillers or human dramas, Taxi Driver offers the perfect balance of excitement and meaning.

👉 Recommended Viewing Order: Start from Season 1 for the backstory, move to Season 2 for high-stakes action, and be ready for Season 3’s grand finale this November!

💡 Final Thoughts

Taxi Driver 3 is shaping up to be one of SBS’s biggest hits of 2025. It’s not just about revenge — it’s about hope, resilience, and fighting for what’s right. If you’re looking for a drama that thrills your heart and challenges your mind, Taxi Driver is a ride you don’t want to miss.
