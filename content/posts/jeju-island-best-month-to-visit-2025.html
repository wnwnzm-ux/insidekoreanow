🌴 Discover Jeju Island — The Perfect Year-Round Getaway

Jeju Island, Korea’s southern gem, is famous for its volcanic landscapes, pristine beaches, and unique local culture. In 2025, it continues to be a top destination for travelers seeking a mix of relaxation and adventure. But to truly enjoy Jeju, timing is everything — let’s find the best month for your visit.

📅 Best Time to Visit Jeju Island

The most recommended seasons are spring (March–May) and autumn (September–November). These periods offer mild temperatures, scenic views, and fewer tourists — perfect for nature lovers and photographers alike. 🌸 Spring (March–May)

Jeju bursts into color with cherry blossoms, canola flowers, and pleasant weather ranging from 12–20 °C. It’s ideal for visiting Seongsan Ilchulbong Peak or walking the Jeju Olle Trail while surrounded by blooms. Spring also hosts Jeju’s famous Canola Flower Festival.

🍂 Autumn (September–November)

Golden fields and clear skies make autumn a favorite among hikers. Temperatures average between 15–25 °C, making it perfect for exploring Hallasan National Park and enjoying the tangerine harvest season. ☀️ Summer (June–August): Beach Lovers’ Season

Summer brings warmth and fun to Jeju’s beaches — perfect for swimming, snorkeling, and kayaking. However, expect higher prices and humidity due to the monsoon season. Popular spots include Hamdeok Beach and Hyeopjae Beach. ([pelago.com](https://www.pelago.com/en/articles/best-time-to-visit-jeju-island/?utm_source=chatgpt.com))

❄️ Winter (December–February): Quiet & Cozy

Winter offers peaceful scenery and cheaper accommodations. Though it’s too cold for beaches, you can enjoy Hallasan’s snow-capped trails or relax in local spas. Temperatures hover around 0–8 °C, ideal for travelers who prefer serenity and fewer crowds.

🧳 What to Expect & Local Tips

  • Weather: Spring and autumn are mild; summer is warm but humid; winter is cold but peaceful.
  • Festivals: Spring brings flowers; autumn brings tangerines and clear hiking days.
  • Accommodation: Book early for April–May and September–October. Prices peak in summer.
  • Transportation: Renting a car gives the most freedom to explore beaches and mountains.

💡 Final Thoughts

For most travelers, the best time to visit Jeju Island in 2025 is from April to May or late September to October. These months offer the perfect balance of weather, scenery, and comfort. Whether you’re chasing blossoms or beaches, Jeju promises something unforgettable every season.

Plan your Jeju trip wisely — and let the island’s beauty surprise you.

💬 Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the best month to visit Jeju Island in 2025?

The best months are April–May and September–October for mild weather, low humidity, and colorful scenery.

2. Is Jeju Island crowded in summer?

Yes, June to August is peak travel season with busy beaches and higher prices. Visit in spring or fall for a quieter experience.

3. Can I hike Hallasan Mountain year-round?

Yes, Hallasan is open all year, but the best hiking conditions are in April–June and October–November when the trails are dry and scenic.

4. Are there typhoons in Jeju?

Yes, Jeju can experience typhoons between late July and early September. Always check the weather forecast before traveling during this period.
