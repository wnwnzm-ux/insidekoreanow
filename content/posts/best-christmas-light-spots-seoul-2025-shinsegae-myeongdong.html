If you are in Seoul right now (December 2025), there is one thing you absolutely must do tonight: see the Christmas lights in Myeongdong. The holiday displays in Seoul have evolved into massive digital art shows, with Shinsegae Department Store leading the trend.

Here is your ultimate walking guide to the best Christmas light spots in Seoul for 2025, including the best photo zones and timing tips.

1. The Main Event: Shinsegae Department Store (Main Branch)

This is arguably the most famous Christmas spot in Korea. The entire facade of the building transforms into a massive screen playing a magical winter movie.

  • Theme (2025): "Magical Holiday Journey" (A whimsical train ride through a snowy village).
  • Best Photo Spot: Across the street, in front of the Seoul Central Post Office. Do not try to stand right under the building; you need distance to capture the whole screen.
  • Getting There: Hoehyeon Station (Line 4), Exit 7. Walk towards Myeongdong.

2. The Classic Vibe: Myeongdong Cathedral

After the dazzling lights of Shinsegae, walk up the hill to Myeongdong Cathedral. The vibe here is calm, spiritual, and romantic. The LED rose garden and the life-sized nativity scene offer a quieter, beautiful backdrop.

3. Bonus Spot: Lotte Department Store (Sogong-dong)

Located just a 5-minute walk from Shinsegae, Lotte Department Store features elaborate window displays (animatronics) and a giant Christmas tree. It feels like a classic European Christmas market.

Recommended Walking Route (Approx. 1 Hour)

To minimize crowd stress, follow this route:

  1. Start at Myeongdong Station (Exit 8).
  2. Walk to Myeongdong Cathedral (Photos: 15 mins).
  3. Walk through the Myeongdong street food alley to Lotte Department Store (Photos: 15 mins).
  4. Walk underground via the shopping center to Shinsegae Department Store (Photos: 30 mins).

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What time do the Christmas lights turn on in Seoul?

For 2025, the lights at Shinsegae and Lotte Department Stores typically turn on at 5:30 PM and turn off at 10:30 PM. Myeongdong Cathedral lights usually stay on until midnight.

Is it crowded? How long is the wait?

Yes, it is extremely crowded, especially on weekends and Christmas Eve. Police and safety staff control the crowd. You might wait 15 to 30 minutes in line just to get to the front row of the photo zone near the Post Office.

When does the Christmas display end?

The displays usually run until January 31, 2026. However, the crowds are thinnest in early January, so visiting after Christmas Day is a smart hack.
