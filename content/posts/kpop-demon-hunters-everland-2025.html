--- id: "kpop-demon-hunters-everland-2025" title: "K-Pop Demon Hunters Festival Opens at Everland (2025 Autumn Special Event)" excerpt: "Everland’s K-Pop Demon Hunters Festival runs from September 26 to November 30, 2025 — featuring mission games, photo zones, costume experiences, and a new sing-along fireworks show that turns the park into a full K-pop fantasy world." dateIso: "2025-10-21" category: "k-culture" status: "published" --- The K-Pop Demon Hunters Festival officially opened at Everland on September 26, 2025, and will run through late November. This large-scale collaboration brings K-pop fantasy to life with immersive games, character experiences, exclusive goods, and special performances.

📍 Event Dates & Location

The K-Pop Demon Hunters Theme Zone runs from September 26 to November 16, 2025, with ongoing stamp and roulette events. You can find the festival at Everland’s Festival Content Zone, located next to Space Tour.

🎮 Main Attractions

The Demon Hunters Theme Zone is divided into interactive experience zones, performance photo areas, and a limited-edition goods shop. Each section offers its own themed mini-games and mission-based challenges.

HuntRix Zone

  • “Crisis Flight” – A flight simulation shooting game that immerses players in fast-paced missions.
  • “Defend the Soul Gate” – A tap-based reaction game where players protect portals by hitting the right spots in time.
  • “Golden” Photo Zone – A performance-style photo booth that recreates the iconic Golden stage background.

Lion Boys Zone

  • “Complete the Song” – A rhythm game synchronized with the original OST, testing your timing and focus.
  • “Offer Your Soul” – A rolling ball challenge where players tilt the board to guide the ball to the target; the hardest of all four games.
  • “Your Idol” Photo Zone – A photo booth featuring the Lion Boys’ stage, complete with reaper hats and idol lighting effects.

🎭 Special Events & Programs

Stamp Mission

Complete all four mini-games to collect stamps. Once the mission is cleared, head to the Reward Zone to receive a random hologram card as a souvenir.

Costume & Makeup Experience

At the popup zone entrance, you can rent character costumes to transform into members of the Demon Hunters world. There’s also a professional makeup truck — the same used for artist tours — where visitors can get styled like Lumi or Jinwoo. The cost is approximately 20,000 KRW, and popular sizes sell out fast.

Limited Goods Shop

The merchandise shop offers 38 exclusive items such as keychains, magnets, cushions, and plush dolls — a must-visit for collectors.

🌟 Tips for Visitors

  • Go Early: Even on weekdays, queues can exceed an hour. Plan an “open-run” to experience popular zones first.
  • Complete Missions: Finishing all four games and collecting all stamps unlocks a unique Irwolobongdo-style hologram card.
  • Photo Spots: The “Golden” photo zone recreates a K-pop concert stage, while the “Your Idol” zone lets you pose as a reaper idol — perfect for Instagram photos.
  • Night Fireworks: After exploring the theme zone, don’t miss the Sing-Along Fireworks Show — a new evening performance that continues the Demon Hunters world from day to night.

🎆 A Day in the Demon Hunters World

During the day, experience thrilling missions and K-pop-inspired mini-games. As night falls, the park lights up for the K-Pop Demon Hunters Sing-Along Fireworks — completing the immersive “Demon Hunters World” experience at Everland.
Event Period: September 26 – November 30, 2025 Location: Everland Festival Content Zone, next to Space Tour, Yongin, South Korea
